Tamil Nadu Electronics Hardware Manufacturing Policy 2020
Electronic Hardware
Tamil Nadu Electronics Hardware
Manufacturing Policy 2020
10 min read
This policy is applicable for EMCs, Private ESDM park developers and projects establishing or expanding industrial units in priority Electronics Hardware Manufacturing sectors in Tamil Nadu , from January 1, 2020.
Contents

1. Policy Targets

2. Financial Incentives for Large/Mega companies

3. Financial Incentives for Park Development

Policy Targets
The Tamil Nadu Electronics Hardware Manufacturing Policy aims to increasing Tamil Nadu’s electronics industry output to USD 100 billion by 2025 ; To contribute 25% of India’s total electronic exports to the world by 2025; To attract at least two major FAB investments to Tamil Nadu in the next three years.
For the purpose of administering incentives
Industries with investment range of INR 200 to 500 crores and employment greater than 500 have been categorised as Large category
Industries with investment range greater than 500 crores and employment greater than 2000 have been categorised as Mega category.

New/expansion units of electronic manufacturing will be provided for a period of 5 years from date on commercial production on power purchased by Tamil Nadu Generation & Distribution Corporation or generated and consumed from captive sources.
